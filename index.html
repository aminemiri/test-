Personal informations

Lionel Andrés Messi Cuccitini was born on June 24, 1987in Rosario , a working-class city in the northeast of Argentina . Third child of Jorge Horacio Messi, worker in a manufacturing plant, and Célia Maria Cuccitini, part-time housekeeper , he has two older brothers, Rodrigo and Matias, and a little sister, María Sol . He has Catalan and Italian origins : Ramon Pérez Llobera, one of his ancestors, comes from El Poal , a municipality in the province of Lleida in Catalonia , while one of his great-grandfathers lived in the city of ' Ancona in Italy, From where he emigrated to Argentina in the xix th century . He therefore has Italian nationality and is registered on the electoral rolls of Recanati. His godfather Claudio Biancucchi is the father of three professional footballers: Maxi , Emmanuel and Bruno Biancucchi. Bojan Krkić , who will be his teammate at FC Barcelona from 2007 to 2011 , is also a cousin to the fourth generation . When he was born, his family resided in Las Heras , a poor suburb in the province of Mendoza , south of Rosario, in a cottage on the edge of a narrow road . He is a particularly shy child and not very talkative, to the point that his primary school teacher advises his parents to go see a child psychiatrist to remedy this. An introvert and little interested in his studies, Messi “transforms” , according to his father, when he plays football . “As soon as he played on a field or in the street, he was no longer the same. At home, he did not speak but with a ball, he looked like he was singing 14 . " His father is a coach and two brothers playing in the Grandoli local club. In 1991, a club coach, Salvador Aparicio, offered him to join a game between two teams of five-year-old players, although he was a year younger. His mother declines, arguing that he is too small and risks hurting himself with the older ones,< but his grandmother urges him to go. Aparicio says:“The first ball passed him beside, on his right, without him reacting. But the second landed directly on his good leg, the left. There, after a check, he left by dribbling a player, then two, three ... I was afraid that the others would hurt him. So I was shouting: shoot, shoot, shoot! But he continued without stopping. I've never seen that. I said to myself: this one, I don't take it out of the team anymore. And I never took it out again . Célia, her grandmother, encourages her and takes her to training. Messi describes a close relationship: “I miss her so much. I so wanted her to see what I have become. >It is for this reason that I dedicate my goals to her, because I like to tell myself that she sees me from where she is. ” . Messi remains for three years at his club. The whole neighborhood comes to see him play . Subsequently, Messi played a few games with Central Córdoba , another Rosario club . despite his health problem he never give up .

achievments

Argentinian footballer Lionel Messi, regarded by many as the greatest player of all time, has received six Ballon d'Or awards, the most for any football player, as well as the 2009 FIFA World Player of the Year and 2019 The Best FIFA Men's Player. Messi holds the record for most goals in La Liga (474), the Supercopa de España (14), the UEFA Super Cup (3) and is the player with the most official recorded assists in football history (315). He has scored 777 goals for club and country throughout his professional career and is also the firstplayer in history to win five and six European Golden Shoes.
